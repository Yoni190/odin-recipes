Bashamel

Description

Egyptian macarona bechamel (مكرونة بشاميل,) is a comfort food recipe popular in Egypt made from penne pasta and a minced meat sauce baked with creamy béchamel. If you’re not already familiar with it, you can think of it as an Egyptian pasta bake with bechamel sauce.

Ingredients

Meat Sauce

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Bechamel Sauce

  • Flour
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Nutmeg

Pasta

  • Penne Pasta
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pasta
  2. Make the Bechamel sauce
  3. Put pasta in a baking dish
  4. Put the meat sauce
  5. Put a layer of mozzarella cheese
  6. Put a layer of the creamy bechamel sauce
